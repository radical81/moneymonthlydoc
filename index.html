Money Monthly

Money Monthly helps you keep track of your expenses. In the main menu, you are given four options: To input your monthly income, track your spending, view your expenses history, and check the status of your monthly expenses.

Awesome Features

Monthly Income

You may enter your current income for the month. It will appear in your income history as you update it. Only the latest income for a specific month will be shown in your history.

Spend

Everything you spend on can be categorised. After creating categories for your expenses, you may start spending.

Add/Edit

You may also edit the category details later on.

Status

You can view a specific category’s limit and percentage spent. By tapping on the circle chart, you may view expenses for a specific category.

Transaction

Each transaction includes the amount, date, and optional description and photo. Keeping a picture of your receipts or dinner is highly recommended.

History

History shows all expenses regardless of category.

Statistics

Stats will help you track your monthly expenses using a pie graph. You can choose to view previous months’ expenses as well.
